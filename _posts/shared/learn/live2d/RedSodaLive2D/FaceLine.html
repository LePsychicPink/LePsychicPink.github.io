Live2D Face 輪廓線

Add mesh

  1. Enable mirror mode

  2. Adjust center line

  3. Use stroke meshing

Reminder: the end of line does not need to touch the center

For reference

  1. Click other tools to confirm

  2. Add center vetices to connect the mirrored path

  3. Add inner vertices along the center line and around the borders

  4. Confirm the mesh

  5. Copy the meshing

  6. Click colore face and edit mesh → paste the copied mesh
